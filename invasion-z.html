About the game

In Invasion Z, you need to slaughter as many zombies as you can get your hands on. But remember to collect ammunition too, otherwise you'll lose out to the horde. Eliminate as many opponents as possible to reach new ranks. As in all arcade games, your score appears on the screen: try to beat your record or even that of your friends.
